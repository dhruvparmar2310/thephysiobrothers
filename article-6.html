All you know about Matrix Rhythm Therapy

Dr Purvi Doshi
(B.P.T.)

As a physiotherapist we are using different kinds of Electromodality. Matrix Rhythm Therapy (MRT) is a one of Advance Mechanoelectrical device.

Matrix Rhythm Therapy (MaRhyThe©) invented by Dr.Ulrich Georg Randoll from Germany has been an innovative therapeutic approach in the field of rehabilitation with minimum or no side effects. Matrix Rhythm Therapy is a vibrational therapy directed at the skeletal musculature.

MaRhyThe© works on the principle that cells in the body vibrate or oscillate in the frequency of 8 – 12 Hz and maintains the physiological function of the body. This therapy stimulates the natural rhythm of the musculature and acts indirectly to regulate the processes coupled with it. As a result, the natural tissue vibration is restored and the cellular logistics are optimized.

Matrix Rhythm Therapy is used in special education and rehabilitation centers; sports clubs; and neurologic, orthopedic, physical therapy, and rehabilitation centers for therapy and diagnosis. Matrix Rhythm Therapy can be used for the improvement of impaired mobility, reduction of swelling, improving elasticity, acute and chronic pain, restoring the function of the microcirculatory network in patients with chronically open wound.

According to researchers Matrix Rhythm Therapy is effective in many conditions and give fast recovery compared to conventional therapies.
